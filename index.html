01  / Origin

From blueprints
to bytes.

I spent years as a Quantity Surveyor inside the Nigerian government sector — quantifying risk, managing multi-million naira procurement pipelines, and producing cost models that underpinned structural decisions.

That work taught me something no bootcamp can: how complex systems actually behave. How data flows. How decisions cascade. How a single miscalculation in a specification document can cost millions downstream.

When I transitioned into tech, I didn't leave that discipline behind. I brought it with me. Today I apply it to digital product architecture — designing interfaces that are not just beautiful, but structurally sound, data-literate, and built to scale globally.
